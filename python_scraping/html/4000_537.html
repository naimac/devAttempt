Joint position of kinematic %1 at end position of segment %2 could not be calculated

Parameter:

%1......

Name of kinematic

%2......

Name of segment

Message Class:

7 -

Reason:

During a check of the target position the corresponding robot joints could not be calculated.

Effect:

The robot is stopped.

Solution:

Use a reachable target position.

Messages

4000_416

Kinematic %1 (joint %2): Setting position not possible. Master axis not referenced (joint %3).

Parameter:

%1......

Kinematic name

%2......

Joint name (whose position should be set)

%3......

Master joint name

Message Class:

4 -Warning

Reason:

The joint's position cannot be set, because the joint depends on another joint (master joint) by a gear, which is not referenced yet.

Effect:

The joint's position has not been set.

Solution:

The master joint must be referenced first.

Messages

56_1113

'%1': Maximum position (%4) of a finite axis exceeded. Consider usage of a modulo axis. (%2, %3)

Parameter:

%1......

name of the axis

%2......

PLCopen state of the axis

%3......

set position

%4......

maximum position of a finite axis

Message Class:

6 -Error Appl

Reason:

Due to limited resolution of LREAL, the maximum position of a finite axis is limited.

Effect:

The axis is in Errorstop.

Solution:

Restart your application and consider usage of axis as a modulo axis.

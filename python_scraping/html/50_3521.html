%1: Joints area '%2' reached, last admissible position not calculable because of passive joints

Parameter:

%1......

Name of robot

%2......

Name of joints area

Message Class:

9 -

Reason:

A joints area has been reached during jogging. The last admissible position at the area border is not exactly calculable due to the passive joints.

Effect:

The given joint cannot move any further in the current direction.

Solution:

Change movement direction or check joints areas.

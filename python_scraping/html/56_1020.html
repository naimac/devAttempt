'%1': Stop Category 2 not supported. Performing Stop Category 1.

Parameter:

name of the axis

Message Class:

5 -Info

Reason:

The drive doesn't support Stop Category 2. Stop Category 1 is applied instead.

A Stop Category 1 stop is performed instead of Stop Category 2.

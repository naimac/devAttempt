Messages

56_5100

'%1': Product jam upstream of Belt %2 (%3).

Parameter:

%1......

name of the infeed

%2......

index of the belt

%3......

-

Message Class:

3 -Error Minor

Reason:

Expected products have not been detected by the sensor (no rising edge). Depending on if removing of products is allowed or not, the products have been removed. A Warning/Error is raised when WarningLevelNrMissedProducts/ErrorLevelNrMissedProducts is reached.

Effect:

-

Solution:

Possible reasons are: a product jam upstream or a sensor malfunction.

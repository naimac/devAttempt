6353_36

%1: Station alias '%2' is used by the slaves at position '%3' and '%4'.

Parameter:

%1......

Configuration path or name of object

%2......

Station alias

%3......

Position of first slave (starting at 1)

%4......

Position of second slave (starting at 1)

Message Class:

3 -Error Minor

Reason:

At least two slaves have the same Station Alias.

Effect:

The operation of the EtherCAT segment is not possible.

Solution:

Configure a unique station alias for this slaves.

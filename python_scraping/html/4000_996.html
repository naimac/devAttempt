Messages

4000_996

Force/torque of joint %2 exceeds limits even if stopped on path at %1

Parameter:

%1......

Segment position

%2......

Joint number

Message Class:

4 -Warning

Reason:

The force or torque limit of the given joint is exceeded even if stopped on path at the given path position. This happens due to tracking or gravity.

Effect:

It could be possible to pass the given path position without exceeding the force/torque limits but it is not possible to stop and therefore it is dangerous to try to pass the position.

Solution:

Increase the force/torque limit or reduce the tracking velocity.

Exceeding cycle time for MCU update (%1 %%)

Parameter:

%1......

Time excess in percent

Message Class:

3 -Error Minor

Reason:

Not enough computing time for the motion interpolator update available in the system.

Effect:

Probably subsequent errors in the micro interpolator update or the drives will happen or the robot will suffer from jerky interruptions of movement.

Solution:

Check configuration and timing of your system. If possible reduce performance of the application or increase the configured cycle time of the motion interpolator. Provide information to your distributor: create a status report and send it to your distributor.

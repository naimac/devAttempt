Kinematic %1: Invalid Position Period of Drive '%2' ([%3;%4[, expected: [-180;180[)

Parameter:

%1......

Name of the kinematic

%2......

Name of the drive

%3......

lower limit of position period

%4......

upper limit of position period

Message Class:

3 -Error Minor

Reason:

The position period of the drive, to which the kinematic should be applied to, differs from -180.0 resp. +180.0.

Effect:

The joint of the kinematic cannot be applied to the drive.

Solution:

The configuration has to be checked.

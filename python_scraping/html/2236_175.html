Messages

2236_175

%1: SERCOS ring not closed.

Parameter:

%1......

Hardware configuration path or name of bus

Message Class:

3 -Error Minor

Reason:

For SERCOS 2: Sercos ring is not closed. Most common reason is, that at least one configured device was not detected. For SERCOS III: No devices detected.

Effect:

Sercos bus bootup not possible.

Solution:

Check of sercos wiring and devices.

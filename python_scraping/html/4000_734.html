4000_734

Provided DH-parameters of robot %1 have at least one redundant joint

Parameter:

%1......

Name of the robot

Message Class:

4 -Warning

Reason:

The configuration of the kinematic leads to at least one redundant joint of the kinematic. Redundant joints are not supported for this type of robot.

Effect:

Kinematic cannot be used. Motion program cannot be started.

Solution:

The configuration of the kinematic (DH-parameter) has to be checked.

Messages

56_5110

'%1': Product exceeded the maximum allowed slippage (%2, %3)

Parameter:

%1......

name of the Infeed

%2......

index of the belt

%3......

detailed error information (TErrorID)

Message Class:

7 -

Reason:

A product has exceeded the maximum allowed slippage. A Warning/Error is raised when correction sensor of the Infeed detected a slippage of a product that exceeds WarningLevelMaxSlippage/ErrorLevelMaxSlippage.

Effect:

-

Solution:

Either the acceleration of the belts in relation to the friction product - belt is too high or the slippage is caused by handing over products from one belt to the next at different belt speeds.

Messages

7458_4

%1: Multi turn resolution of encoder %2 is reduced from %3 to %4 bits (%5 bits single turn resolution)

Parameter:

%1......

Configuration path or name of (S)MM36x

%2......

Encoder number (1-6)

%3......

Multi turn resolution of encoder

%4......

Reduced multi turn resolution

%5......

Single turn resolution of encoder

Message Class:

5 -Info

Reason:

The sum of single turn and multi turn resolution exceeds the maximum bit count of 31 bits. The multi turn resolution is reduced.

Effect:

The drive detects a position overrun earlier.

Solution:

none

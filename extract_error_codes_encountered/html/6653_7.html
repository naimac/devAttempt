Messages

6653_7

%1: Incompatible version %2 of sercos3-FPGA

Parameter:

%1......

HW-configuration path of card

%2......

Version of sercos3-FPGA

Message Class:

3 -Error Minor

Reason:

Incompatible sercos3-FPGA. No automatic update available, it may be necessary to update the FPGA as part of another FPGA (f.e. System-FPGA).

Effect:

The Sercos3-card can not be operated.

Solution:

Manual Update of another FPGA (f.e. System-FPGA) necessary.

4000_346

AutoStop-job %1 cannot be applied to segment %2

Parameter:

%1......

Name of job

%2......

Name of segment

Message Class:

3 -Error Minor

Reason:

An autostop-job cannot be assigned to a segment because the corresponding path does not support autostop-jobs.

Effect:

The segment will not be appended to the path.

Solution:

Remove autostop-job, use different limiter or activate look-ahead.
